The Problem
1
Manual Annotation Is the #1 AI Bottleneck
Human annotators take 15-45 minutes per long-context document. At $0.10-$2.00 per label, enterprise-scale annotation costs millions annually and cannot scale with model data demands.
2
Long-Context Exacerbates Inconsistency
Inter-annotator agreement drops below 70% for complex tasks spanning 4K+ tokens. Fatigue, ambiguity, and cognitive load produce noisy labels that degrade model performance.
3
No Unified Cross-Task Solution Exists
Current tools are task-specific: NER annotators cannot do classification, QA labelers cannot evaluate code. Teams juggle 5-10 specialized tools, fragmenting workflows and increasing overhead.
4
The Market Is Exploding
The global data annotation market is projected to reach $10.3B by 2028 (CAGR 28.2%), driven by LLM training demands. Supply of qualified annotators cannot keep pace with demand.
