The Problem
1
Manual Annotation Is the #1 AI Bottleneck
Annotators take 15-45 min per long-context document. At $0.10-$2.00 per label, enterprise annotation costs millions and cannot scale.
2
Long-Context Worsens Inconsistency
Inter-annotator agreement drops below 70% for 4K+ token tasks. Fatigue and cognitive load produce noisy labels that degrade models.
3
No Unified Cross-Task Solution
NER tools cannot classify. QA labelers cannot evaluate code. Teams juggle 5-10 tools, fragmenting workflows.
4
Market Exploding: $10.3B by 2028
28.2% CAGR driven by LLM demands. Supply of qualified annotators cannot keep pace with demand.
