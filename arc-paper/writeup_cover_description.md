## Design Rationale

The cover image visually represents the core AnnotateX concept: an input grid undergoes a structured transformation to produce an output grid. The left grid shows a simple 3x3 pattern with colored cells arranged in a recognizable geometric shape (such as an L-shape or diagonal), while the right grid shows the result of applying a rotation and scaling transformation. A directional arrow between them symbolizes the inference pipeline.

The blue and white color scheme conveys a professional, academic aesthetic consistent with IEEE-format research publications. The minimal style avoids visual clutter and keeps focus on the abstract reasoning concept. The 16:9 aspect ratio (1344x768) matches the standard Kaggle Writeup cover image dimensions.
